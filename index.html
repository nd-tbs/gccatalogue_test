Helping government teams deliver better services

The GC Catalogue is a curated set of reusable digital and data policy tools, case studies and precedents to help unblock government teams trying to deliver secure, easy-to-use and trustworthy services.

Digital and Data Policy Tools

Accessibility

Accessibility statement

A way for Government of Canada organizations to describe how they're meeting accessibility requirements, and what work still needs to be completed

Procurement requirements generator

A prototype wizard to help departments generate procurement requirements for use in contracting

Sample contract language

Language to embed in procurement contracts to ensure that services meet the GC's accessibility requirements

Privacy

Information-sharing agreements involving personal information

A written record of understanding between parties that are sharing personal information

Sample privacy notice

A template privacy notice for Government of Canada institutions that can be modified as needed

Privacy breach management toolkit

A toolkit with templates to help teams respond to privacy breaches
