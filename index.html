Helping government teams deliver better services

The GC Catalogue is a curated set of reusable digital and data policy tools to help unblock government teams trying to deliver secure, easy-to-use and trustworthy services.

You can begin using the tools today, or reach out to our team for help getting started.

Categories

Accessibility

Accessibility statement

A way for Government of Canada organizations to describe how they're meeting accessibility requirements, and what work still needs to be completed

Procurement requirements generator

A prototype wizard to help departments generate procurement requirements for use in contracting

Sample contract language

Language to embed in procurement contracts to ensure that services meet the GC's accessibility requirements

Privacy

Information-Sharing Agreements Involving Personal Information

A written record of understanding between parties that are sharing personal information

Sample privacy notice

A template privacy notice for Government of Canada institutions that can be modified as needed

Privacy breach management toolkit

A toolkit with templates to help teams respond to privacy breaches

Talent and Leadership

C-suite roles

Descriptive language for c-suite roles for digital and data

Job descriptions

Descriptive language for job descriptions for digital jobs for reuse

Organizational structures

Description: Descriptive language for potential organizational structures

User Research

Compensation form

Sample compensation form to be used as proof that user research participants were compensated for their time

Consent and privacy notice generator

A prototype consent and privacy notice generator to support with user research design sessions

Service Canada Labs

An experimental corner of Canada.ca where government teams can test and get early feedback to improve their digital policy or service

We’re here to help

If you're unsure of how to use any of the tools in this catalogue, or if you'd like to contribute a tool for consideration contact us.

We look forward to hearing from you and will respond promptly.

The GC Catalogue is a community

The GC Catalogue is a collaboration between the Office of the Chief Information of Canada and Service Canada. Together, we represent a community of public servants committed to supporting government. Share your thoughts and suggestions with us by e-mail servicedigital-servicesnumerique@tbs-sct.gc.ca.
